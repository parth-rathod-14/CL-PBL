About Arena

Deogiri's Arena is a college sports event management platform created to organize and promote exciting indoor and outdoor sports competitions. The website helps students explore schedules, register for events, and view winning teams easily. Our goal is to encourage teamwork, fitness, leadership, and healthy competition among students.
